# Healthcare Operations and Cost Analytics: A Data-Driven Study of Patient Admissions and Outcomes

## Project Charter

### Background

Healthcare systems generate large volumes of operational and financial data. Understanding patient admissions, medical conditions, insurance coverage, and healthcare costs can help improve decision-making, operational efficiency, and resource allocation.

This project analyzes a healthcare dataset containing patient demographics, admission details, medical conditions, insurance providers, and billing information to uncover meaningful insights that can support healthcare management and policy decisions.

### Problem Statement

Healthcare providers must balance quality patient care with operational efficiency and cost management.

Understanding the drivers of healthcare utilization, treatment outcomes, and billing costs is essential for improving service delivery and resource allocation.

This project investigates patient admissions, treatment outcomes, insurance coverage, and billing patterns to identify factors that influence healthcare utilization and costs.

### Project Objectives

#### 1. Analyze Patient Demographics

* Examine age and gender distributions.
* Explore blood type patterns.
* Identify demographic trends within the patient population.

#### 2. Investigate Healthcare Utilization Patterns

* Analyze admission types.
* Examine the prevalence of medical conditions.
* Identify patterns in healthcare service utilization.

#### 3. Examine Healthcare Costs

* Evaluate billing amounts across patients.
* Identify high-cost conditions and admission categories.
* Assess cost variability across healthcare services.

#### 4. Explore Insurance Provider Trends

* Analyze insurance provider representation.
* Compare billing patterns across insurers.
* Investigate potential cost differences among insurance groups.

#### 5. Identify Operational Insights

* Explore relationships between admissions, conditions, and outcomes.
* Assess healthcare performance indicators.
* Highlight operational improvement opportunities.

#### 6. Develop Data-Driven Recommendations

* Provide actionable insights for healthcare decision-makers.
* Recommend opportunities for cost optimization.
* Support evidence-based resource allocation strategies.
